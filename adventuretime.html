Fionna And Cake Season One
11Fionna Campbell

Fionna Campbell dreams of fantastic adventures while living with her cat Cake. Cake acts strangely, leading Fionna to the vet, where she gets fired and eventually meets Ellis P. Cake then chases a mysterious blue light and disappears into an ice cream stand.

12Simon Petrikov

Simon Petrikov works in a 20th-century styled home for visitors in Ooo and is tormented by his past as the Ice King. A ritual to revive Betty opens a portal that brings Cake into Simon's world.

13Cake the Cat

Prismo ignores alarms in the Time Room. Cake flees with Simon's voice translator, causing chaos in Ooo's market. Fionna searches for Cake with the help of Astrid, and they are teleported away via another portal.

14Prismo the Wishmaster

Prismo teleports Fionna, Cake, and Simon to the Time Room, revealing the hidden Fionna & Cake universe. Scarab investigates, and Prismo gives the trio a remote to escape. Simon discusses his Ice King status with them.

15Destiny

The trio searches for the crown in Farmworld and meets Jay, Finn's son. They find crown shards removed by Prismo and taken by the Destiny Gang. Scarab captures Prismo and is teleported with the trio, taking a shard to fix Prismo's remote.

16The Winter King

Gary meets Marshall Lee for a baking project. Simon, Fionna, and Cake meet the Winter King (an alternate Simon). The Candy Queen kidnaps both Simons. Fionna saves Simon, deactivates the crown, killing the Winter King, and lifts the curse while fleeing from Scarab.

17The Star

In an alternate reality, Cake joins Bonnie and her vampire-hunting team. The raid fails, and Simon leaves with Fionna and Cake. Gary and Marshall attend a charity ball, and Gary leaves with Marshall Lee after a disagreement.

18Jerry

In a lifeless alternate reality, BMO lives alone before meeting the trio. Fionna finds old tapes about the Ice King's madness. BMO dies, and they find "Jerry," actually the Lich. Simon resumes a ritual to return Fionna and Cake to their universe, but they fall into a red portal.

19Casper & Nova

Fionna and Cake return to their universe. GOLB crushes the Lich and transfers Simon’s consciousness into Shermy. Simon investigates a choose-your-own-adventure book with Beth, finds clues, while Scarab invades Fionna's world but is freed by Ellis P.

110Cheers

Scarab attempts to erase Fionna's world. Simon uses game decisions, dooming Nova. Before returning to their universe, he gives Fionna a magical dandelion. Fionna-world becomes authorized, Scarab works for Prismo, and Fionna and Simon stay in contact via a multiversal phone line.
